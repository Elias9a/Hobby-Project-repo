About

Manchester United Football Club, is a professional football club based in Greater Manchester(north east of England), England.
The club competes in the Premier League, the top division in the English football league system. Nicknamed the Red Devils, it was founded as Newton Heath LYR Football Club in 1878, but changed its name to Manchester United in 1902.
